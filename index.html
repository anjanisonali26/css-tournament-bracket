Getting Started

Create the 8 players and 16 players tournament bracket. Image thumbnail of each player included.

How to Use CSS Tournament Brackets

  1. Include bracket.css on your side

    <link rel="stylesheet" href="bracket.css">

  2. Add Markup to your HTML

    Add your images for the player at your_image. For 8 player-tournament, you can center the bracket which is class="side-...". r-1 stands for round one â€“ r-2 , r-3 , r-4 as well.
